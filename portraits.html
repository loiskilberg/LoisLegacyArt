Commissioned Portraits

Lois enjoys creating commissioned portraits for people to cherish moments of their lives, from pictures of places lived and pets remembered to portraits of loved ones. Send Lois your photo and discuss how you would like the portrait to be done. Small changes can be made to the photo. You can choose color (in watercolor) or black-and-white (in charcoal). A deposit and a photo is all that is needed to get started. Click here to request a portrait commission!
