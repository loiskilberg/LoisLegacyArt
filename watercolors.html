Watercolor Paintings

Lois is inspired by a range of subjects from still life to historical buildings and landmarks to landscapes. She brings her sense of fine art realism into her illustration pieces. Lois is available for commissions.

For A Musician

This watercolor painting was commissioned by Lois' father, who was a musician. Each element has personal meaning, from the violin to the flute to the "Magnificat" organ manuscript.

Water Tower, Arlington MA

This watercolor painting depicts the water tower in Arlington, Massachusetts. It has a unique design. The water tower is visible from Route 2 heading into Boston.

Parking Problem

This watercolor and digital illustration brings out humor in a situation. Lois combined traditional watercolor techniques with digital design.
